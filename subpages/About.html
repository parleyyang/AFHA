About
The group focuses on functional analysis and harmonic analysis applications in a broad sense. Topics of interest include:

  • Complexity and Computability Theory
  • Compressed Sensing
  • Computational Harmonic Analysis
  • Computational Spectral Theory
  • Computations in infinite dimensions
  • Inverse Problems
  • Kinetic Theory
  • Mathematical Signal Processing
  • Medical Imaging (MRI and CT)
  • Multiscale Problems
  • Sampling Theory
  • Spectral Theory and Ergodic Theory
  • Statistical Modelling
  • Time Series Analysis
